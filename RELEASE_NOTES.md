# 🎛️ DJ Mixer — v3.0.0 "Club Mode"

**A full two-deck DJ console in your browser — now with a proper club-rig makeover.** Dark Pioneer-style CDJ decks, spinning jog wheels, a stacked beatmatch waveform, and a central DJM mixer. Load YouTube links, audio URLs, your own files, or built-in beat loops. Real beatmatching, a library that remembers, and one-click crate-digging. No installs, no logins.

▶ **Try it now:** https://sudockin.github.io/dj-mixer/

---

## 🆕 What's new in v3.0 — the CDJ + club-mixer redesign

- 🖤 **Dark club theme** — a full re-skin from a light desk to a stage-ready rig
- 🌊 **Stacked beatmatch waveform** — both decks overlaid with a center playhead, so you can *see* the beats line up
- 💿 **Jog wheels** — a spinning turntable per deck with live BPM + time in the hub
- ⏯️ **CDJ transport** — dedicated **CUE** and **PLAY/PAUSE**
- 🎚️ **DJM central mixer** — per-channel **EQ kill** buttons (HI / MID / LO), **vertical channel faders + VU meters**, **PFL** cue, crossfader with Smooth / Linear / Cut
- 🗂️ **Collapsible "+ Load Track"** — the source panel tucks away for a cleaner deck

Everything you already loved is intact: **SYNC + continuous phase-lock**, **Auto-sync**, cue / loops / quantize, the **Music Library** (history, favorites, search, drag & drop), the **Queue** (auto-advance), and **Discover** — including one-click **→ Deck A/B** loading straight from a suggestion.
